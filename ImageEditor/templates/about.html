About Skibidi

Skibidi is a unique web app which provides a myriad of image manipulation options such as image blurring, black and white filters, converting to pdf, adding grayscale effect, and resizing the image. It's built with Django, a Python web framework.

Our Mission

We strive to get a 20 on this project by implementing various concept that Professor Igor Peretta taught us in the Software Engineering class.
